What to Wear

Guest Dress Code

We're going for Garden Formal — elegant enough for a special occasion, relaxed enough to dance. Think flowing fabrics, earthy tones, and florals. The ceremony is on grass, so stilettos are best left at home.

A-Line

Classic & universally flattering

Midi Wrap

Maxi / Column

Timeless & glamorous

Smart Suit

Sage, linen or warm tones

Please avoid

  • White, ivory & champagne — reserved for the bride
  • Black — opt for deep forest, navy or charcoal instead
  • Stiletto heels — ceremony and dancing are on grass

Come Prepared

What to Pack

June in the Yarra Valley is beautiful but changeable. Here's what we suggest:

  • A warm jacket or wrap — evenings get chilly
  • Block heels, wedges or flats for the grass
  • Sunscreen & sunglasses for the afternoon ceremony
  • A small umbrella — June can bring light showers
  • Overnight bag if staying on the estate
  • Camera — we'd love your candid shots
  • Cash for optional winery tours Sunday
  • Your very best dancing shoes
